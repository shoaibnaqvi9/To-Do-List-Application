About Our Application

Welcome to our Todo List Application! This web application allows you to organize your daily tasks with ease. Users can sign up, log in, and manage their to-do lists effortlessly. Whether you're planning your week or tracking progress, this app provides you with a user-friendly way to stay on top of your tasks.

Key Features

  • Secure user registration and login.
  • Create, edit, and delete to-do list items.
  • Filter tasks based on their status (active, completed).
  • Simple, clean, and responsive interface using Bootstrap.

Our Objective

Our primary objective is to provide a streamlined and efficient platform for managing daily tasks. We aim to empower users to stay productive, organized, and on schedule, all while maintaining a pleasant user experience.
